Hobbies

Golf

Golf is something that is relaxing and frustrating at the same time. It requires patience to learn and get better at. I had grown up playing soccer all of my life but in the last few years I have had some re-occurring injuries that have kept me from playing so I have began playing golf much more. My wife and friends all play so it is something that we can enjoy together and I one day hope to teach my children to enjoy the sport, I know they are both eager to come out and play with their mom and dad.

Traveling

This one is a newer hobby. As a family, we have not been able to travel much due to the ages of the children but with them getting older it is something that we have been able to do more, and hopefully can continue to do. It will never be easy with two children and also two adults that can all have strong personalities, but when we get to create those memories it is all worth whatever frustrations we have endured.

Family

It would have been easy to put some other items in this place. I enjoy playing video games, or used to before this program started, board games, puzzles, trying new whiskeys and cooking. But with the growth of our family it is hard not to have these times take up more space within our lives every passing year. Our children have started playing sports and doing activities none stop it feels like, and at times it seems like my wife and I do not have time for each other, let alone ourselves. She is a great mother and has sacrificed so much in this last 6 months with taking the kids to many of their activities on her own to allow me to complete homework and studying. It is hard not to say that family is a hobby these days because these are such fun times to watch our kids grow and see them develop into their own personalities. There are definitely times that I have missed, but the ones I am able to see and share are priceless.
